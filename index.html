Health Tracker

This is an application for users to track the mental & physical health of themselves and/or someone in their care. Examples of this are elderly, children, someone with special needs, etc... A user has the ability to track their day to day mental and physical health, as well as the variables and occurrences that may have attributed to their condition that day.
